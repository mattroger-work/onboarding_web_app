Welcome to TekSynap!

Please find the attached TekSynap Employee Orientation for you to review. The presentation will cover a variety of topics.

Your login information for your TekSynap email account will be sent to you by our IT Team. All TekSynap sites utilize the same sign-in credentials including Unanet (timesheet) and ADP (payroll system). Beginning Monday, July 27, 2020 you will be able to login to ADP by going to the TekSynap Employee Portal https://www.teksynap.com/employee-portal/ and clicking on the "ADP - Employee & Managers" tab. We recommend using Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome when using ADP.

You will complete the onboarding process and benefit enrollment in ADP. Employees can enroll in benefits by navigating to Myself>Benefits>Enrollments.

As part of the onboarding process, you will be asked to complete an online version of the I-9 Form in ADP. Additionally, the I-9 form must be completed within 72 hours of your start date with TekSynap, by July 29th Due to your location, you will need to schedule an offsite I-9 verification appointment via Fieldprint. You will receive an email invitation to schedule your I-9 appointment at a Fieldprint location convenient for you.

Because TekSynap utilizes E-Verify, you are required to input your social security number at the time of your Fieldprint Appointment. Please be sure to include your Social Security Number while completing your I9 form at Fieldprint or you will be required to complete this process an additional time. You will need to bring the appropriate I-9 document(s) so you can verify your identity. A list of acceptable documents will appear on page 3 of the attached I-9 form.

If you cannot make an appointment within the 72 hour of your start date period please email hr@teksynap.com and we will assist you with other options.

If you have any questions after reviewing the presentation please feel free to reach out.
